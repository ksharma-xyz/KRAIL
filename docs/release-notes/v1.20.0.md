# KRAIL v1.20.0 — Release Notes

## Android — Google Play Store

v1.20.0

🏷️ Your stop names follow you. Named a stop "Home" or "Work"? Those labels now show on your saved trip cards and in journey results — no more decoding raw station names.

🕐 See earlier and later trains. Tap "Show Previous" or "Load More" at the top and bottom of any timetable to browse departures beyond the default window.

📌 Reorder your saved trips, and they stay. Drag them into your preferred order — KRAIL now remembers it between sessions.

Let's KRAIL.

## iOS — App Store

v1.20.0

Your stop names, everywhere.

Named a stop "Home" or "Work"? Those labels now appear on your saved trip cards and in journey results — clean and clear.

Earlier or later, your call. The timetable now lets you scroll back to see previous departures, or load more upcoming services ahead.

Trip order remembered. Rearrange your saved trips and the order sticks — exactly how you left it.

Fixed: Changing the transport mode filter now correctly refreshes your trip results.

Let's KRAIL.
